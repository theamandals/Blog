Rather than buying materalistic things, If I do have to buy something, then 99% of the time it's on food. I love not only listening to music, but also playing music as well. Lastly, I not only love to travel, but also learn about languages and culture. So far I've traveled to 5 countries (China, Canada, Haiti, Virgin Islands, and Sanya), and I hope to be given the opportunity to travel the world.
